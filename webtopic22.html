    • Disadvantages of Computer Science in healthcare

    

    Even though Computer Science can bring great rewards to healthcare, there are a few negative effects. 

     1. HIGH COST OF IMPLEMENTATION
    
        Setting up AI-based machines, computers, etc. There are immense costs involved due to the complexity of 
        engineering that goes into building one. 

    2. RISK OF UNEMPLOYMENT
        
        AIs are likely to take over the majority of the repetitive tasks which could be mundane jobs carried out 
        by humans currently. Job cuts could pose a serious strain on families in the future.active.
        AIs could result in a less human intervention which may cause major disruption in employment. Most 
        organizations are implementing automation to replace the minimum qualified individuals with machines 
        that can do the same work with higher efficiency. In healthcare, AI machines could be in control of 
        all the admin work.

    3. DOESN’T IMPROVE WITH EXPERIENCE

        Human cognitive power has an ability to develop with age and experience. However, AIs are machines that
        can’t improve with experience, it starts to wear and tear with time.
        Whenever there is some change in the input, the AIs have to be completely re-assessed, re-trained and 
        re-build. Furthermore, machines don’t have a moral compass and can’t judge what is right or what is wrong
        because they are incapable of understanding the concept of morality. They are programmed for certain 
        situations and won’t be able to make a decision in cases where they encounter an unfamiliar situation 
        since they aren’t programmed for that.
